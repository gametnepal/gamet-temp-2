What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is an open environment that doesn't come built-in with structured quests and other limitations. This means the players can roam through this world and explore without an urgent set of tasks. It's a sandbox game Thet begs for exploration and self discovery.

The skill of experimentation and problem solving will help people master challenges in Science, Math and many more subjects. It exercises huge amounts of imagination due to its sandbox nature and increases future opportunities for creativity.

By switching into multiplayer mode, players can work together to build and co-create whole new worlds. There has also been scientific research that found that while playing game people suffered less anxiety and and pain caused by medical procedure and illness. Prestige from hosting the games attracts trade and tourism.

We organisers are trying to contribute to the community on our scale.
